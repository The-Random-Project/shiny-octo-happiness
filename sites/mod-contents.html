Modded Minecraft is when people add their own creations to Minecraft, such as mobs, items, tools, food, blocks, etc.
My friend (FlickerAway) likes it because apparently "It adds/fixes stuff that Mojang messed up."
Welcome to the world of Modded Minecraft!

Here is a list of mods and modpacks you should try!

  • RLCraft (by Shivaxi)
    RLCraft stands for Real Life Craft, so expect this to be the hardest mod you've ever played, because real life is harder than anything else.
    Click here to go to our tutorial!
  • New Items (by Chekin (One of the creators of this website))
    Adds a bunch new items, and is one of the best mods ever, if I say so myself! (eheheh)
    Coming from an unbiased source: don't listen to them
    Click here to go to our tutorial!
  • ATM6 (by ATMTeam)
    Has All The Mods (not really, but close enough), so is actually pretty easy to play
  • Quark (by Vazkii)
    Adds everything that Mojang should have, INCLUDING VERTICAL SLABS!!!!
  • Roguelike Dungeons (by Greymerk)
    A mod that adds giant dungeons underground with lots of good loot!
  • XLife (by DAQEM, ragequitamy, and SuperSlice06)
    You start with 1 heart, and everytime you die, you get 1! But once you get 10 hearts, that's your last life~
  • Oh the Biomes You'll Go (by AOCAWOL and Corgi_Taco)
    Adds over 80 new biomes!! Try to find them all!
  • Better Minecraft (by SharkieFPS)
    This is basically Minecraft 2.0! Adds tons of new biomes, mobs, items, etc.
