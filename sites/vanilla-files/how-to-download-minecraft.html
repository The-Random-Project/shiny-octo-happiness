In order to play Minecraft, you need to have Minecraft! Follow these steps in order to buy Minecraft.
Step one: Go to   minecraft.net
Step two: Click the green “Get Minecraft”
Step three: Choose your device (I’m going to choose Computer, Windows, Java Edition for this example.)
Step four: Click the green “Buy Minecraft” (You won't have bought it yet though)
Step five: Sign into your Mojang/Microsoft account, or create one if you don’t have one yet. This should be fairly fast.
Step six: Fill out the form and push “Purchase!” Congratulations, you have purchased Minecraft and are now ready to play.
